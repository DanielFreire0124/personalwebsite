Where I am From

Lush Rainforests, Turquoise Beaches, Land of Soccer and Carnival. You guessed it I am from Brazil. I grew up in Sao Paulo, and made many great memories there, from spending summers at my aunt's farm in the rolling hills of Minas Gerais, to pulling all-nighters with my family to watch carnival festivities. In 2017 my parents took their chances and moved to the United States, where i have lived since.
